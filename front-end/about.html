Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions answered

How quickly are bundles activated?

Bundles are typically activated instantly after payment confirmation. In rare cases, it may take up to 5 minutes. If you experience any delays, contact our support team.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect all transactions and personal data. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

What if the bundle doesn't activate?

If your bundle doesn't activate, please contact our support team immediately with your transaction ID. We'll investigate and provide a refund or resend the bundle within 24 hours.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer refunds for unused bundles within 24 hours of purchase. Some promotional bundles may have restrictions. Contact support for details.

Do you offer corporate packages?

Yes! We offer special bulk pricing and corporate packages for businesses and organizations. Contact our business team for customized solutions.

How can I track my transaction?

You can view all your transactions in the History section after logging in. Each transaction includes details like date, amount, bundle type, and status.
